Website Planning Document

This site plan outlines the purpose, target audience scenarios, color scheme, typography, and home-page wireframes for the High Ground Odyssey website project.

1. Site Name

Site Name: High Ground Odyssey

Reason: “High ground” is a meaningful phrase to my brother (a combat veteran), and Odyssey fits our theme of long-form storytelling and adventure.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a hub for our podcast / YouTube channel / writing project. It will:

  • Feature the latest episode, including the portion of the book we are writing that week.
  • Include an “About” page so visitors can learn who we are and what we’re building.
  • Provide coaching details, a newsletter signup, and a contact option.

3. Scenarios

  1. Where can I find the latest episode (and past episodes)?
  2. How do I sign up for coaching, and what plans are available?
  3. Who are the creators of High Ground Odyssey, and what is the project about?

4. Color Scheme

The color palette aims for a cinematic teal-and-orange mood (cool shadows + warm highlights) inspired by our Journey.jpg hero illustration and the current HighGroundOdyssey.com branding. The “parchment” background keeps long-form reading comfortable.
